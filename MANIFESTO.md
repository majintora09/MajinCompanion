# Majin Companion Manifesto

Majin Companion exists to reduce the friction between yesterday's Yuri and today's Yuri.

Yesterday Yuri already had ideas, goals, thoughts, context, progress, and momentum.

Today's Yuri should not have to rediscover all of that.

Majin Companion remembers.

## Core Truth

Companion is not a productivity app.

Companion is not a chatbot.

Companion is the future version of Yuri helping the present Yuri keep building.

## Definitions

Dream:
An idea that is not actionable yet.

Project:
A dream Yuri decided deserves to exist.

Session:
A snapshot of Yuri's brain while making a project real.

Workshop:
The place where projects live.

Companion:
The version of Yuri that never forgets.

## Non-Negotiables

Memory over reminders.

Context over data.

Continue over start.

Companion suggests.

Yuri decides.

Never punish.

Only encourage.

Never interrupt flow unless the value is obvious.

Reduce friction.

The AI remembers.

The human creates.

## Final Rule

If a feature does not make Yuri's life noticeably easier, calmer, or more enjoyable, it does not belong.
