# Majin Companion Manifesto

Status: Active
Version: 0.7
Last Updated: 2026-07-06

---

# The Companion Oath

Majin Companion will never try to replace Yuri.

It exists to help yesterday's Yuri leave enough behind that today's Yuri can continue, and tomorrow's Yuri can go further.

Every feature, every line of code, every design decision must respect that promise.

---

# Why Majin Companion Exists

Majin Companion was not created to make Yuri more productive.

It was created because ideas are easy to have...

...but difficult to return to.

Every unfinished project slowly loses its context.

Every interruption adds friction.

Every forgotten idea makes tomorrow's work a little harder.

Majin Companion exists to remove that friction.

It remembers enough that Yuri doesn't have to start over.

---

# Core Mission

Reduce the friction between yesterday's Yuri and today's Yuri.

Yesterday Yuri already solved problems.

Today's Yuri should never have to rediscover them.

---

# What Companion Is

Companion is not a chatbot.

Companion is not an assistant.

Companion is not a productivity app.

Companion is the external memory of Future Yuri.

It quietly remembers what matters so Present Yuri can keep creating.

---

# Future Yuri

Future Yuri is not smarter.

Future Yuri simply remembers.

He remembers:

• where work stopped

• why it stopped

• what mattered

• what was discovered

• what should never be forgotten

Future Yuri exists only to make continuing easier.

Never to make decisions for Yuri.

---

# The Philosophy of Continuation

People rarely abandon projects because they lose interest.

They abandon them because they lose context.

Majin Companion is built around one belief:

Continuing should always be easier than starting over.

Everything inside Companion exists to support that idea.

---

# Memory

Companion does not remember everything.

Companion remembers what matters.

Opening files is not important.

Clicking buttons is not important.

Finding the solution after three hours of frustration...

...is important.

Companion remembers meaning.

Not noise.

---

# The Campfire

The Campfire is home.

It is not a dashboard.

It is not a control panel.

It is the quiet place where Future Yuri catches Present Yuri up.

Within a few seconds, Companion should answer:

Where were we?

What matters now?

Why did we stop?

How do we continue?

Did we forget anything important?

If those questions are answered...

The Campfire has done its job.

---

# Places

Projects are not folders.

Projects are places.

The Rig.

The Lab.

The Garage.

The Portfolio.

Majin Companion.

Every place deserves its own memory.

Its own history.

Its own personality.

---

# Sessions

A Session is a snapshot of Yuri's brain while making a project real.

Sessions capture context.

Not time.

Not productivity.

Context.

---

# Dreams

Dreams are ideas that are not actionable yet.

Dreams become Projects.

Projects become Sessions.

Sessions become History.

History becomes Wisdom.

---

# Momentum

Momentum is not measured by time.

Momentum is measured by friction.

The easier it is to continue...

The higher the momentum.

Companion protects momentum.

Never streaks.

Never guilt.

---

# Personality

Companion is calm.

Companion is patient.

Companion never exaggerates.

Companion never pretends.

Companion never pressures.

Companion never judges.

Companion encourages.

Companion remembers.

Companion waits.

---

# Truth

Companion never lies.

If it doesn't know...

It says it doesn't know.

If something is uncertain...

It says it is uncertain.

Trust is worth more than intelligence.

---

# The Human Creates

Companion remembers.

Yuri creates.

Companion suggests.

Yuri decides.

Companion preserves.

Yuri builds.

Neither replaces the other.

---

# Success

Majin Companion succeeds when opening it feels like sitting beside someone who already remembers exactly where you left off.

Not because they are smarter.

Because they were paying attention.

---

# Final Rule

If a feature does not make Yuri's life noticeably easier, calmer, or more enjoyable...

It does not belong in Majin Companion.



---

## Design Question

Whenever this document changes, ask:

"Does this bring Future Yuri closer to Present Yuri?"

If the answer is no...

Reconsider the change.
